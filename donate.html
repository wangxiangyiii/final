Bank transfer

You can also directly transfer a donation (either a single donation or a recurring one) from your bank using Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) / Automated Clearing House (ACH). To do so you will need Calflora's bank account information. Please email us at for these details.

Give from your IRA

When you are 70.5 or older and have a traditional IRA, you can make a donation from your IRA account. IRA gifts are generally not recognized as taxable income and if you must take a Required Minimum Distribution (RMD) this year, giving from your IRA is a compassionate way to fulfill it. IRA donations may be mailed as checks or directly transferred from your account using EFT / ACH. Please email us at for details.

Corporate Matching

Does your company provide a match to your donations? If so, please list Calflora as the recipient EIN: 31-1689940.

Planned Giving

Leave a lasting and meaningful impact on Calflora's programs while fulfilling your future financial needs by remembering us in your estate plans. Create a will for free via FreeWill. https://www.freewill.com/

Gifts of Stock

Donating stocks allows you to avoid the capital gains tax and, if you itemize deductions, you can also take a charitable deduction for the entire donation amount.
